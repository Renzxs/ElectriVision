What is ElectriVision?

Empowering a safer and more efficient campus at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela, ElectriVision is a dedicated electrical reporting system designed to streamline issue reporting and maintenance. Our platform enables users to quickly report electrical problems, ensuring prompt attention from campus staff and improved safety for all.
